Active To Passive

Simple Tense

Active : She watches a movie.
passive : A movie is watched by her.

Active : They beat him last night.
Passive : He was beaten by them last night.
Active : He will buy a car.
Passive : A car will be bought by him.
Active : They must arrest the culprits.
Passive : The culprits must be arrested by them
Active : They florist delivers flowers to my office everyday.
Passive : Flowers are delivered by them florist to my office everyday.
