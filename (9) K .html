L.M.Montgomery


L.M.Montgomery (1874-1942) was a canadian author best known for a series of novels beginning in 1908 with Anne of Green. Montgomery went on to publish 20 novels as well as 530 short stories,500 poems,and 30 essays .A prolific writer,Montgomery published over 100 stories between 1897 and 1907.Montgomery's work,diaries and letters have been read and studied by scholars and readers worldwide.
