Active to passive

Perfect Tense
  1. Active : Shanthi has completed her work.
  2. Passive : Shanthi work has been completed by her
  3. Active : We had made a mistake.
  4. Passive : A mistake had been made by us.
  5. Active : They will have built this building by next may.
  6. Passive : This building will have been built by them by next may
