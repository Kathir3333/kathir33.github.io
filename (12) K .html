Slogan

Slogans are short phrases. They are used in taking any message to the general public. This is written in the advertisments and the campaigns for public information or enlightenment.
These slogans are to be as far as possible short,meaningful,intersting and capable of being attractive in their choice of words,sentences and lay-out.
These slogans help us when we have to spread message during the social awareness programmes and when we take the points to the general public throwing light on the feature of the products advertised and the points focussed.
Through this exercise the learners acquire to use words appropriately,correctly and attractively and also in an effective manner.
